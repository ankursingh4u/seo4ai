# SEO4AI — Product & Go-To-Market

**Live:** https://seo4ai.bolddev.live
**One line:** SEO4AI tells you whether AI assistants (ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, Claude) recommend your brand — scores your AI visibility, shows where competitors beat you, and gives you the exact fixes.

---

## 1. The shift we're built on
People used to *Google* "best CRM for startups." Now they *ask ChatGPT* — and the AI names 3 brands. If you're not one of them, you're invisible, and you'll never even see the lost traffic in your analytics.

This new discipline has a name: **GEO / AEO** (Generative / Answer Engine Optimization) — "SEO for AI." It's where SEO was in ~2004: real, growing fast, and most brands have no idea where they stand. SEO4AI is the measurement + action layer for it.

## 2. What we provide
- **AI Visibility Score (0–100)** — how often AI recommends you across real buyer questions.
- **Multi-prompt testing** — we run the actual questions customers ask (not vanity queries).
- **Competitor gap analysis** — see exactly who AI recommends instead of you, and by how much.
- **Missed searches** — questions where rivals appear and you don't (your hit-list).
- **AI Fix Plan** — prioritized, specific actions to climb (Max).
- **Boost content generator** — ready-to-paste FAQ, brand bio, key facts, and JSON-LD schema that help AI learn about you (Max).
- **Progress tracking + competitor alerts** — visibility over time; know when you slip or a rival surges.
- **Shareable report cards** — every report renders a branded score card on social.
- **Region targeting** — results tuned to a country/state/city market.

## 7. How to get the first 100 customers
The product's output **is** the pitch. Lead with the result, not the features.

### Phase 1 — First 1–10 (this week, founder-led, $0)
1. **Pick ONE narrow niche** (e.g., Shopify skincare brands, or B2B HR-SaaS, or med-spas in one city).
2. **Scan 30–50 of them** yourself; note score + who beats them.
3. **Send the result, not a pitch:**
   > "I tested what ChatGPT recommends for [niche]. [Competitor] showed up 9×; you showed up 1×. Full breakdown: [report link]. Want the 5 fixes?"
4. Expect 3–8 replies, 1–3 first users. If zero → fix the niche/message, not the product.

### Phase 2 — To ~50 (content + community, organic)
5. **Public teardowns / leaderboards:** "I asked ChatGPT 25 buying questions about [niche] — here's who it recommends 👇." Post on LinkedIn, X, r/SEO, r/marketing, Indie Hackers, niche Slacks/Discords. These travel because the angle is hot and the data is concrete.
6. **SEO content** targeting the exact searches your buyers make: "is my brand on ChatGPT," "how to rank in AI search," "GEO checklist." (Blog already seeded — expand it.)
7. **Shareable score cards** (already built) — every user who shares becomes a billboard.

### Phase 3 — To 100 (channels + launch)
8. **Agency partnerships** — agencies resell AI-visibility audits to their whole client base. One agency = many brands. Offer a white-label/affiliate deal.
9. **Product Hunt + directories** (BetaList, AI tool directories, Indie Hackers) — time it with a strong demo GIF of the scan.
10. **Cold-email at scale** — productize Phase 1 with the batch-outreach tool (paste 50 brands → 50 personalized emails).

## 8. Most efficient marketing (ranked by ROI for *this* product)
1. **The free scan as a weapon (cold + warm).** Personalized "your competitor beats you" outreach converts because it's true and specific. Cheapest, highest-converting. **Do this first.**
2. **Shareable report cards (built-in virality).** Zero cost, compounds — every share markets you.
3. **Public leaderboards / teardowns (content marketing).** SEO + social reach + brands self-promote their rank. Best organic flywheel.
4. **Build-in-public on LinkedIn/X.** The GEO topic is trending; founder posts about AI-search findings get reach for free.
5. **Agency channel (partnerships).** Highest leverage for revenue — one partner brings many paying brands.
6. **Community seeding** (Reddit/IndieHackers/Slack) — genuine, helpful posts with a free scan offer.
7. **Paid ads — LAST.** Only after the message/niche is proven via 1–6. Retarget free-scan users who didn't sign up.

**Rule of thumb:** spend nothing on ads until 30 personalized cold scans have produced real interest. The free scan + shareable cards + teardown content can realistically carry you to the first 100 with $0 in media spend.
