# AuraRank - Feature Test Guide

## What is AuraRank?
AuraRank tells you whether AI tools (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Gemini) are recommending your brand when people search for your industry. It gives you a visibility score, shows where competitors beat you, and tells you exactly how to fix it.

---

## Quick Test (No Login Required) - 2 minutes

1. **Open the homepage**
2. Enter any brand + industry in the free scan widget. Try:
   - Brand: `Notion` | Industry: `project management`
   - Brand: `Freshworks` | Industry: `CRM software`
   - Or use your own brand name
3. Hit scan and wait ~10 seconds
4. You'll see:
   - **AI Visibility Score** (0-100) - how often AI mentions this brand
   - **Prompt breakdown** - which AI prompts mentioned the brand and which didn't
   - A nudge to sign up for the full 20+ prompt report

---

## Full Test (With Account) - 5 minutes

### Step 1: Sign Up
- Click "Get Full Report" or go to `/signup`
- Enter email + password (min 8 characters)
- Check your email and click the confirmation link

### Step 2: Add Your First Brand
- You'll land on the dashboard with a brand setup form
- Fill in:
  - **Brand name** (e.g. your company name)
  - **Industry** (e.g. "CRM software", "Italian restaurant", "cloud hosting")
  - **Website** (optional)
  - **Competitors** - add up to 3 competitor names
  - **Region** - pick Global, or narrow it to Country/State/City
- Save and your first scan starts automatically

### Step 3: View Your Results
After ~20-30 seconds, your dashboard shows:
- **Visibility Score** - your overall AI visibility (0-100)
- **Brand Mentions** - how many AI prompts mentioned you (e.g. 8/20)
- **Competitor Gap** - how you stack up against competitors
- **Quick Wins** - prompts where competitors appear but you don't (your opportunity)

### Step 4: Explore Features
- **Competitor Chart** (Pro+) - bar chart comparing your mentions vs competitors
- **Trend Line** - track your score over time across multiple scans
- **Prompt Opportunities** - specific prompts to target for better AI visibility
- **AI Fix Plan** (Max) - actionable recommendations with priority + difficulty ratings
- **Share Report** - generate a public link to share results with your team

## What to Look For

- **The free scan on the homepage works instantly** - no signup friction
- **Dashboard is clean and actionable** - score + gaps + what to fix
- **Regional targeting** - scans are tailored to your market (city/state/country)
- **Competitor gap analysis** - see exactly where rivals outperform you in AI results
- **AI-generated fix plan** - not just data, but specific steps to improve visibility
